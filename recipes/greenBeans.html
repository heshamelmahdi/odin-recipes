Air Fryer Green Bean Fries

Description

Green bean fries get extra crispy when cooked in the air fryer. Fresh green beans are first parboiled, then seasoned, air fried, and served with a special sauce.

Ingredients

  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • Green beans

Steps

  1. Whisk together olive oil.
  2. Preheat air fryer
  3. arrange beans in an even layer in the air fryer
  4. All done!
